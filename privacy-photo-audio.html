📸 Privacy Policy - Photos and Voice Messages

Last updated: March 12, 2026

This policy supplements the main JukeBox App Privacy Policy and specifically applies to the sending of photos and voice messages via QR Code.

⏱️ In summary: Photos and voice messages are stored for a maximum of 14 days, after which they are automatically and irreversibly deleted.

1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how JukeBox App processes multimedia files (photos and voice messages) sent by the public through QR Code-generated public pages. This feature allows fans to share photo memories and voice messages with performers during live events.

2. Data Collected

2.1 Photos

When you send a photo through a public QR page, we collect:

  • Image file: The photo you choose to share
  • Technical metadata: File type, size (max 10MB), date and time of submission
  • Caption (optional): Text accompanying the photo
  • Sender name (optional): The name you entered in the "Your name" field
  • Device information: Device type (mobile/desktop), operating system, browser (for statistical and debugging purposes only)

2.2 Voice Messages

When you record and send a voice message, we collect:

  • Audio file: The voice recording (max 10 seconds)
  • Duration: Actual recording time
  • Technical metadata: File type, size (max 512KB), date and time of submission
  • Sender name (optional): The name you entered in the "Your name" field
  • Device information: Device type (mobile/desktop), operating system, browser (for statistical and debugging purposes only)

2.3 Consent Data

To ensure GDPR compliance, we collect and store:

  • Consent timestamp: Date and time you accepted photo/audio submission
  • Anonymous device fingerprint: Non-reversible technical identifier (contains no personal data)
  • Anonymous technical information: Device type, operating system, browser, screen resolution (for aggregate statistics)
  • QR session ID: Identifier of the QR code used

Note: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information except the optional name you choose to provide.

3. Purpose of Processing

Photo and audio files are collected exclusively for:

  • Delivery to the performer: Allowing the artist to receive and view content sent by fans during the event
  • Event memory: Creating a collection of memories for the performer (visible only to the artist)
  • Service improvement: Anonymous statistical analysis of feature usage

Files are not used for:

  • Marketing or profiling
  • Algorithm or AI training
  • Sharing with third parties (except as necessary for hosting)

4. Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of your photo and audio files is based on:

  • Your explicit consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): Provided via the checkbox in the consent modal before submission
  • Performance of a service (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): Allowing the performer to receive the content you wish to share

5. Data Retention - 14 Days

⏰ Retention period: 14 DAYS

5.1 Photos and Voice Messages

All photo and audio files are stored for a maximum of 14 days from the date of submission. After this period:

  • ✅ Files are automatically deleted from our servers
  • ✅ Deletion is irreversible and files cannot be recovered
  • ✅ Both original files and any backups are removed

This limited retention policy ensures that your content does not remain in our systems longer than necessary.

5.2 Consent Data

Consent-related data (timestamp, device fingerprint) is stored for 14 days in the database, aligned with file duration. After this period, it is also automatically deleted.

5.3 Exceptions

The only exception to deletion after 14 days occurs if:

  • The performer manually exports files before expiration (creating a local copy)
  • Legal obligations require longer retention (e.g., judicial investigations)

In these cases, original files are still deleted after 14 days and only exported copies remain under the performer's responsibility.

6. Storage Location and Transfers

Photo and audio files are stored on servers located in:

  • European Union (Frankfurt, Germany) - Google Cloud data centers

We do not transfer your files outside the European Union. For anonymous technical data (device fingerprint, statistics), processing may occur on servers in Europe.

7. Sharing with Third Parties

7.1 Service Providers

We use the following services to host and manage files:

  • Google Cloud Storage (Firebase): We host photo and audio files on Google Cloud infrastructure, located in Europe. Google acts as a data processor and processes data according to their Data Processing Terms.

7.2 Data Recipients

Your files may be viewed by:

  • The recipient performer: The artist who generated the QR code
  • Authorized JukeBox personnel: Only for technical assistance or maintenance (limited and tracked access)

Files are not shared with other third parties, sold, or used for purposes other than those described.

8. Your Rights

Regarding the photo and audio files you submit, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: You can request information about which files you have submitted (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification: You can request to modify the caption (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten): You can request early deletion of your files, before the 14-day period (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing: You can request to limit processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to object: You can object to processing (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability: You can request a copy of your files in a common format (Art. 20 GDPR)

8.1 How to exercise your rights

To exercise your rights, contact us at: privacy@jukeboxapp.eu

You will need to provide:

  • The QR code ID used (if available)
  • Approximate submission date
  • File description (to help us identify it)

We will respond within 30 days of the request.

9. Security

We adopt technical and organizational security measures to protect your files, including:

  • Encryption in transit: All files are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Encryption at rest: Files are encrypted on servers
  • Access controls: Only the recipient performer and authorized personnel can access
  • Access logs: All file access is tracked and monitored
  • Limited backups: Backups follow the same 14-day deletion policy

10. Minors

The service is not intended for individuals under 13 years of age. If you are a parent and discover that your child has submitted photos or audio without your consent, contact us for immediate removal.

For minors between 13 and 18 years, photo/audio submission is only permitted with the consent of a parent or guardian.

11. Changes to this Policy

Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page. In case of substantial changes, we may provide additional notice (e.g., via in-app notification).

The update date at the top indicates the latest revision.

12. Contact

  • Email: privacy@jukeboxapp.eu

  • Data Protection Officer (DPO): dpo@jukeboxapp.eu
